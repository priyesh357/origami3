Interesting Facts about Chameleon

  • Chameleon are reptiles that are part of iguana suborder
  • Changing skin color is an important part of communication among Chameleon
  • Most Chameleon have a prehensile tail that they use to warp around tree branches

Interesting Facts about Pigeon

  • Pigeons are reowned for their outstanding navigational abilities
  • Pigeons have excellent hearing abilities
